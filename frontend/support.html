Frequently Asked Questions

How does the predictive AI work?

Our Machine Learning model (Random Forest) was trained on dataset patterns analyzing Storage Duration, Quantity, and Storage Facility Type (Fridge vs Room Temp). It determines if food is Fresh, Expiring, or Waste instantly.

How do I donate food?

If you are an Admin, you have access to the "Donation Zone" on your dashboard. When food shows up as 'Expiring', physical boxes drop out of the sky. Just click and drag them into the Donation Bin!

What is the difference between Admin and User?

Users are generally restaurants, supermarkets, or individuals adding inventory. Admins represent the logistics managers coordinating pickups, viewing global statistics, and confirming donations.
